ABOUT BPIE

    Bishnupur Public Institute Of Engineering(Polytechnic College), established in 2010 in the lap of nature, is accommodated in well-lit, airy, spacious five edifice on lush green, hygienic, pollution free, 15 acre campus. It has exotic gardens with vast games and sports areas. The premises are vibrant with the whistling of birds, whispering breeze and mesmerizing fragrance of flowers.
    This Polytechnic College has state-of-the art laboratories and workshops. It has excellent Computer Labs and Language Lab. The libraries are equipped with a huge range of books, journals and magazines on various subjects and disciplines. The Institute has dedicated, experienced and highly qualified faculty members who provide quality education to students that meets global standards. B.P.I.E. stands for excellence; its magnificent infrastructure has few parallels in west Bengal.
